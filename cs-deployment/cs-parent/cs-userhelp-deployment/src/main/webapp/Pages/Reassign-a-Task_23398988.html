Sirma Platform : Reassign a Task

Tasks may be reassigned to another user.

Preconditions

Only active tasks may be reassigned.
Only the assignee of the task and the originator of the workflow may reassign a task.

  • The assignee of a task (1) opens it, may enter Comments (2) and then selects the action "Reassign Task" from the section of the possible actions on the task (3-4).

Note: The operation may be started also from Personal Dashboard/ My Tasks dashlet or from Case Dashboard /Tasks dashlet .

  • The system opens the screen and the user is able to select from a drop down  menu or after a search another user as an assignee of the task (1-2). In the list are only these users who are members of the same group as the former assignee of the task.

  • The selected new assignee is moved in the right section (1). Selected users may be deselected clicking the correspondent "-" button.
  • The user clicks the button "Save" (2) and the task is sent to the selected new user.

  • The task is not visible for the former assignee on his/ her Personal Dashboard/ My Tasks dashlet or in Case dashboard/ Tasks dashlet.
  • The individual rights of the former assignee of "Collaborator" on the case, change to "Consumer". When this user is a member of a group with "Collaborator" permissions for the corresponding type of case, these permissions remain.
  • The new assignee of the task gets "Collaborator" permissions on the case and the task appears on his/ her personal dashboard (1-2).

  • If the task is opened after the reassignment, it is visible that all attributes of the task remain unchanged, except for the assignee of the task.
